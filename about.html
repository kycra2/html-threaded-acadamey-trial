About Threaded Treasures Academy

Where passion meets tradition in the art of quilting

Our Story

Founded with a deep love for the traditional craft of quilting, Threaded Treasures Academy was born from the belief that handmade treasures carry stories, memories, and love that cannot be replicated by machines.

Our academy represents more than just learning techniques â€“ it's about preserving a beautiful art form that has brought families together for generations while inspiring new creative expressions for the modern quilter.

Every stitch we teach, every pattern we share, and every project we guide carries forward the rich heritage of quilting while embracing contemporary innovation and artistic freedom.

Our Mission

To preserve and celebrate the timeless art of quilting while inspiring creativity and fostering a community of passionate crafters who value handmade beauty and tradition.

Our Vision

To be the premier destination for quilting education, where students of all skill levels can discover their creative potential and master both traditional and contemporary techniques.

Our Values

Quality craftsmanship, patient instruction, creative expression, community building, and the preservation of traditional skills for future generations.
